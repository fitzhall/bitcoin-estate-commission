Bitcoin Estate Planning Standards (Draft)
=========================================

This is a placeholder for the PDF lead magnet.

To activate the email capture system:

1. Replace this file with your actual PDF:
   - Name: bitcoin-estate-planning-standards-draft.pdf
   - Location: /public/downloads/

2. Configure your email service provider by adding to your .env.local:
   - For Resend: RESEND_API_KEY=your_api_key
   - For ConvertKit: CONVERTKIT_API_KEY=your_api_key

3. The email capture is already integrated on:
   - Homepage (after the hero section)
   - All city pages (after the horror stories section)

For more information, visit: https://bitcoinestatecommission.org
